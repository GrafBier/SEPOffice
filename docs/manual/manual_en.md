## 1. Introduction
SEPOffice is a lightweight, high-performance office suite designed for privacy and speed. Every document you create is stored locally, and our assistant **Eliot** runs directly on your machine.

---

## 2. SEPWrite - The Intelligent Editor
SEPWrite is more than just a word processor. It features:
- **AI Completion**: Start typing, and let Eliot suggest the next words.
- **Tone Optimization**: Highlight text and ask Eliot to make it more professional, friendly, or concise.
- **Rich Formatting**: Full support for headings, lists, quotes, and images.

![SEPWrite](../assets/sepwrite.png)

---

## 3. SEPGrid - Dynamic Spreadsheets
Manage your data with ease using SEPGrid:
- **AI Formula Generation**: Just describe what you want (e.g., "Sum columns B and C if A is Paid"), and Eliot writes the formula.
- **Virtualization**: Handle massive datasets with 10,000+ rows smoothly.
- **Visualizations**: Create charts and tables instantly.

![SEPGrid](../assets/sepgrid.png)

---

## 4. SEPShow - Captivating Presentations
Create stunning slides in minutes:
- **Magic Layout**: One click to arrange your elements perfectly.
- **Vibe Check**: Let Eliot review your slides for visual consistency.
- **Export to PPTX**: Presentation-ready files for any audience.

![SEPShow](../assets/sepshow.png)

---

## 5. Eliot - Your AI Assistant
Meet **Eliot**, the heart of SEPOffice. You can chat with Eliot in every app.
- **Context Aware**: Eliot knows what you're working on.
- **Action Driven**: Ask "Add a table with budget info" or "Insert a summary of this document," and Eliot will do it for you.
